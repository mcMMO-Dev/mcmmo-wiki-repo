# Spears

Spears is a combat skill focused on fighting with spears. As you level up Spears, you gain passive damage bonuses and combat mobility effects.

## XP Gain

XP is gained by hitting mobs or other players with a spear.

## Spear Mastery

**Ranks:** 8

Spear Mastery is a passive sub-skill that increases damage dealt with spears. The bonus damage scales with rank, rewarding dedicated Spears users with increasingly powerful strikes.

## Momentum

**Ranks:** 10

Momentum is a passive sub-skill that gives you a chance to gain Speed II when you hit a target with a spear. The chance to activate and duration of the speed effect both scale with rank. Momentum will not override a stronger or longer-lasting Speed effect already applied to you.

## Spears Limit Break

**Ranks:** 10

Limit Break is a passive sub-skill that provides bonus damage against tough opponents in PvP. The bonus scales with rank. It can optionally be enabled for PvE via `Skills.General.LimitBreak.AllowPVE` in the advanced config (disabled by default).

## Commands

Use `/spears` to view your Spears skill information in-game.
