# Smelting

Smelting is a skill that improves your furnace efficiency. Level it up by smelting items in furnaces to unlock bonuses to output, fuel consumption, and vanilla XP gain.

## XP Gain

XP is gained passively whenever items finish smelting in a furnace.

## Sub-Skills

### Second Smelt

**Ranks:** 1

Second Smelt is a passive sub-skill that gives you a chance to receive an extra smelted item whenever a furnace completes a smelt. The chance scales with your Smelting skill level.

### Fuel Efficiency

**Ranks:** 3

Fuel Efficiency is a passive sub-skill that increases how long furnace fuel burns. Each rank multiplies the burn time of fuel:

| Rank | Burn Time Multiplier |
|------|---------------------|
| 1    | 2×                  |
| 2    | 3×                  |
| 3    | 4×                  |

### Understanding the Art

**Ranks:** 8

Understanding the Art is a passive sub-skill that multiplies the vanilla Minecraft XP earned from smelting. Higher ranks provide greater multipliers to vanilla smelting XP.

## Commands

Use `/smelting` to view your Smelting skill information in-game.
